Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 6, 2026

Fun Pomodoro ("the App") is a productivity timer application developed by Farindra Project. This policy explains how we handle your information.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you sign in with Google via our authentication provider (Keycloak), we receive your name, email address, and profile picture. This information is used solely to identify your account and sync your settings across devices.

Usage Data

The App stores your pomodoro session history (timer mode, duration, focus tags, and timestamps) locally on your device using a SQLite database. This data is not sent to any external server.

Settings

Your app preferences (work duration, break duration, theme, focus tags) are stored locally and optionally synced to your account via Keycloak for cross-device access.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Authentication: To verify your identity and provide account access.
  • Settings sync: To save and restore your preferences across devices.
  • Session history: To display your productivity statistics locally on your device.

3. Data Storage & Security

  • Session history and settings are stored locally on your device.
  • Authentication tokens are stored securely using platform-specific secure storage (Android Keystore).
  • Settings sync uses HTTPS encryption for data in transit.
  • We use Keycloak as our authentication provider, hosted at keycloak.farindra.com.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Google Sign-In: Used for authentication. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Keycloak: Self-hosted identity provider for authentication and settings sync.

We do not use any analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Your session history never leaves your device.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

You can delete your session history at any time from within the App. To delete your account and all associated data, contact us at the email below.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

9. Contact
